ABOUT US

INSTA-KITCHEN is customized and designed to the smallest detail to suit your individual needs, to reflect your lifestyle and fit your budget! ALL AT THE SAME TIME AND IT’s NOT JUST KITCHENS :Since 1997 , INSTAKITCHEN has been a fore-runner in the world of MODULAR KITCHENS , WARDROBES and other bespoke furniture for home and hearth. With over 5000 satisfied customers, INSTA-KITCHEN is one of the few companies that manufacure their own components in a modern, wood-working factory using the world’s best machinery and inputs sourced from all over the globe.

An INSTA-KITCHEN product, with an ISO9000:2008 QUALITY certification and prompt after-sales service Is an investment for a life-time.
